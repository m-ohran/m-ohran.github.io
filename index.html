I grew up in Orem, Utah, about 5 minutes away from Utah Valley University, where I was one of the first students to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Bioinformatics. I'm passionate about learning and science and have been interested in biology and the natural world since I was quite young. That grew into an interest in evolution and genomics, which made UVU's Bioinformatics program a natural fit for me. While at UVU I had the opportunity to get involved in undergraduate research, and found that I really enjoy it. I greatly enjoy problem solving and discovery, and the challenge of searching for answers is something I find really exciting.
